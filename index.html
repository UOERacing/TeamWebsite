ABOUT US

Who Are We

We are a group of engineers dedicated to designing and building the most efficient Formula 1 Electric cars. The purpose of the team is to apply knowledge gained in the classroom to real life problems. Our team consists of first year University of Ottawa students from a diverse group of engineering and business majors. We also have faculty advisers providing advice and guiding us towards our mission. Our focus is to promote STEM and compete in Formula SAE events.

What is SAE

Formula SAE (FSAE) is a design competition organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) International. It was started in 1979 by the University of Michigan. SAE is a global professional association that provides standards for the engineering profession and donates money to promote STEM education. The idea behind the competition is for individual universities to field a fictional company to design, build, and race a small open wheel race car for amateur autocross competitions. Teams are judged upon dynamic and static events, where the static portion focuses on design and marketing, while the dynamic portion focuses on racing. FSAE is highly competitive and extremely awarding, as it allows students to learn much more than in the classroom and to obtain networking opportunities from the world's best engineering firms.
