About Us

We are a group of engineers dedicated to designing and building the most efficient electric cars. The purpose of the team is to apply knowledge gained in the classroom to real life problems. Our team consists of first year University of Ottawa students from a diverse group of engineering and business majors. We also have faculty advisers providing advice and guiding us towards our mission. Our focus is to promote STEM and compete in Shell Eco Marathon and SAE events.

Shell Eco Marathon

The Shell Eco-marathon Drivers’ World Championship brings together the world’s best UrbanConcept teams competing in an exciting race to see who the world’s fastest energy-efficient driver is.

Formula

The SAE International Formula SAE program is an engineering design competition for undergraduate and graduate students to enhance their engineering design and project management skills by applying learned classroom theories in a challenging competition.
